LITSCO has thrived on the philosophy as quality manufacturers of sheet metal for panels and edge metal and and distributors for roofing, waterproofing and sealants in the commercial construction industry. A four generation commitment to the industry, you can trust we will help you meet your construction challenges for fabrication or distribution anywhere in the U.S.
We keep your building protected from the outside, in: The physical components of the envelope include the foundation, roof, and walls. LITSCO is able to tie in all of these components as a single source supplier. We have teamed up with key vendors to supply you with the best products for roofing, waterproofing, metal panels, air-vapor barrier and sealants in the industry. When installed properly, these products will keep your building protected from the elements. Work with us and we will be glad to help you decide which product will be best for your specification.
