  • filter_dramaArchitectural Metal Panel Project Manager
    Overview:

    LITSCO focuses on precision fabrication of aluminum facade panels and value-added distribution of leading pre-formed metal panel systems from leading manufacturers including LITSCO's Streamline© Panels, Centria, Fabral, ATAS, PAC-CLAD and more. A primary focus of the position will be to promote, market, and sell our Streamline© metal panels and commercial panel products by maintaining and expanding company’s customer and architectural specification base. Additionally, the candidate will manage the sales and estimating of projects for the metal panel projects, and therefore, the ability to negotiate contract terms and pricing with vendors and manufacturers will be part of the role.

    The candidate will oversee projects from the initial request for bid, to clarify scope of work, coordinate with our fabrication team for scheduling and output, and negotiate costs and delivery dates with vendors. In addition, position will require project site visits for field assistance related our in-house staff for detailing and development of shop drawings. There will be a major role to work across account lines within existing accounts, as well as identifying new opportunities, create relationships with architects and building owners to create and develop specifications, act as liaison to manufacturers’ reps, and provide quotations to prospects. Delegates day to day office operations to appropriate personnel, and required to perform all duties in the absence of personnel.

    This is a full-time position.

    Requirements:
    • 3+ years proven metal panel sales experience at architectural and project level.
    • Minimum 2 years college or military service.
    • Technical understanding of rainscreen systems and total wall assemblies including insulation and air barriers, ability to read architectural specifications and drawings, and comprehension of metal building components.
    • Proficient computer skills and familiarity with Estimating Software, Microsoft Office products such as Word, Outlook and Excel.
    • CSI Certification a Big Plus!
    • Self-motivated with discipline to follow up leads.
    • Experience with CRM methods.
    • Ability to work with variety of people including those with English as second language.
    • Solid verbal and written communication and listening skills.
    • Valid Driver’s license and insurable driving record.

  • filter_dramaAccount Manager
    Overview:

    Known for our technical expertise in the roofing, waterproofing, and architectural metal panel industries, LITSCO focuses on distribution of commercial building products to top quality professional contractors. Due to our close relationships with our vendors, such as Johns Manville, Soprema, Firestone, Carlisle Coatings (CCW), Laurenco, and Fabral, we can be more than just order takers: clients come to us for advise on plans, specifications, and detailing of their projects. Our team focus on value-added services such as boom truck deliveries to the roof top, in-house tapered insulation design and metal shop drawings, managing project logistics such as material inventory, and accurate client billing.

    The candidate must have proven experience in developing account leads, ensuring success for the entire lifecycle using a CRM, track and follow opportunities, strong history in sales of building products including the understanding of pricing from vendors and to clients, knowledge of the building envelope assemblies from the foundation to the roof, and be able to read plans and specifications. The position requires handling incoming inquiries from customers via phone, email, fax, or walk-ins, as well as from internal references. Additionally, it is expected to be able to drive to and from client appointments and job sites. You will learn about how to build and develop client relationships to ensure a rich experience for your accounts. Experience with commercial roofing and waterproofing products is a plus!

    This is a full-time position.

    Requirements:
    • 2+ years construction sales.
    • 2+ years of college or military service.
    • Self-motivated, independent, reliable.
    • Ability to work with variety of people including those with English as a second language.
    • Strong communication skills - both verbal and written.
    • Strong computer skills and familiarity with Microsoft Office and Bluebeam.
    • Experience with CRM methods a plus!
    • Experience reading specifications and plans.
    • Valid Driver’s license and insurable driving record.
